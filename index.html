We're currently making Lasertag

Lasertag (one word) turns any space into a laser tag arena with a few Meta Quests and the press of a button. It's an experimental multiplayer mixed-reality FPS that 'just works.'

Right now we're focusing on core features, so the game is pretty bare bones. There are also many bugs.

We recommend playing on Quest 3 or 3s, as older headsets lack features important to the game.

Known issues

  • Moving too far will interrupt the game with a system pop-up.
    • You can disable 'physical space features' in system Settings > Advanced > Enable Custom Developer Settings > Physical space features.
  • Headsets must be on the same network and subnet to connect.
    • Enterprise networks may put devices on different subnets and prevent connection.
    • You can use a phone or Windows PC hotspot if your network isn't working.
  • Meta's shared anchors are finicky and often fail.
  • Players' virtual avatars will drift and dislocate from their real bodies over time.
  • Playspace map syncing will occasionally cause crashes.
  • Depth-lights are unoptimized.
  • Distant lasers and avatars are invisible due to limited depth-occlusion range.
  • The playspace mapper will add other players' bodies to the heightmap.
