anaglyph

We're currently making Lasertag

Lasertag (one word) turns any space into a laser tag arena with a few Meta Quests and the press of a button. It's an experimental multiplayer mixed-reality FPS that 'just works.'

Right now we're focusing on core features, so the game is pretty bare bones. There are also many bugs.

We recommend playing on Quest 3 or 3s, as older headsets lack features important to the game.

Be sure to read the in-game manual!
